What is Make My Schedule?

It's exactly what it sounds like: A Chrome extension that automatically creates your dream schedule! (and every other possible schedule, in case you're picky like us ðŸ˜‰)

How do I start?

First, download the Make My Schedule extension on Chrome. Pay a small price of $3.99. Follow simple instructions to select your school, and you're ready to go!

How do I use it?

In Make My Schedule, type in your term and class names. Use settings and sliders to fine-tune your preferences. Then, click "Go to Registration" to open your school's registration site. All the classes available will be read automatically. Every possible schedule will be generated, and sorted to your liking. You can flip through options with the number boxes on top.
When you find one you like, open Make My Schedule and click "Save Schedule". Now, you can view, register, and plan this schedule.
